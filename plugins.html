Useful Plugins/Scripts

Plugins and scripts to make your work even more powerful.

Flow ↗

It is a script that helps users apply easing and graph adjustments faster and more easily in After Effects.

Un-PreCompose ↗

Un-PreCompose reverses the built-in After Effects function “Pre-compose,” making it easier to separate layers again.

FX Console ↗

It is a script that allows users to access and apply effects more quickly inside After Effects.

Deep Glow 2 ↗

It is a plugin that creates a more pleasing glow than the built-in glow effect in After Effects.
